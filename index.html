FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Phishnix.

Do you support prototype PCB orders?+

Yes — prototyping is our specialty. We support single-piece and small-batch prototype orders with fast turnaround, typically 3–7 days for standard specs. Express options available for urgent needs.

Can you help with component sourcing?+

Absolutely. We handle full BOM sourcing — ICs, passive components, connectors, modules, and specialty parts. We work with verified distributors to ensure quality and availability.

Do you support EV and IoT hardware?+

Yes, these are core focus areas for Phishnix. We support BMS boards, motor controllers, power electronics, BLDC drivers for EV applications, and wireless/sensor hardware for IoT products.

What file formats do you accept?+

We accept Gerber files (RS-274X), Excellon drill files, BOM in Excel/CSV, pick-and-place files, and assembly drawings in PDF. We also work with KiCad, Altium, and Eagle native formats.

Do you provide assembly support?+

Yes. We offer full PCBA services including SMT, through-hole, mixed technology, BGA rework, and functional testing coordination for simple boards to complex multi-layer assemblies.

Can startups work with you?+

Phishnix is specifically built for startups. We offer flexible quantities, no large MOQ requirements, transparent pricing, and a startup-friendly process from MVP to pre-series A stages.
